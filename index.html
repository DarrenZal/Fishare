Fishare

  

A Fair way to share fish

   Register   Claim Quota   Trade   Fish Market   Transfer  

Fishare is a fishery network.

Fishers can trade the rights to catch a share of fish.

Anyone can trade fish in a virtual market.

Note: The software runs on the Ethereum blockchain (Ropsten testnet), and currently requires a web3 supported digital wallet such as Metamask.
